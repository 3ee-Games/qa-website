Code of Conduct

  • Published: Mon, Dec 13, 2021
    • Have a positive attitude and be kind to everyone. Remember that other folks see the world differently from you and assume you're in the wrong if someone calls you out.
    • Let people like what they like; don't hate on the preferences of others.
    • Profanity is to be used sparingly and as a tool for positive emphasis, not as a weapon to hurt people.
    • Don't skate the line of breaking the rules. When you're in doubt, you've broken the rules.
    • If you get called out for breaking the rules, thank the caller for the heads up, reflect, tighten up, and carry on.
    • No blatant advertising, spam, scams of any kind. If you want to share your creations with others, do it in a meaningful way.
    • Any homophobic, transphobic, racist, sexualized, political content is not allowed. We love freedom of speech and the first amendment. However, hate speech, racism, and hating on the preferences on others will not be tolerated. Our games are for everyone and anyone.
    • Cheating isn't a good look. We don't ban cheaters though: we match up cheaters with other cheaters so everyone feels like a god. Then we collect and sell their tears on our onlyfans page.
    Consequences : Moderators can issue timeouts and other ways of curbing unwanted behavior in our games and community. If you receive a ban from any of our games, there is a chance you'll be banned from our Discord server and other games as well. Bans are not handed out lightly and we believe in giving second chances.

Reporting bad conduct

Report violations of 3Games’ Code of Conduct by contacting us. Do not feel ashamed for reporting other players that are harressing and/or threating you. We are committed to providing fun adventures for everyone.

Did you make a mistake or regret your past behavior?

If you currently have limited account access and are ready to appeal your account restrictions, contact us. Everyone makes mistakes. 3Games is forgiving, even for repeat offenders. We all have bad days. Sometimes a string of bad days. All we ask is that you do not take advantage of our forgiveness and enjoy our games while treating everyone with respect.
